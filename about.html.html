My Journey

The Story Behind RankVibe

I started my digital marketing journey with one simple belief — every business deserves to grow. Growing up in Delhi NCR, I saw countless local businesses struggle to compete online while larger brands dominated the search results. That frustration became my fuel.

After years of working with agencies and learning the craft from the inside out, I founded RankVibe — a digital marketing agency that combines the power of artificial intelligence with deep human expertise to deliver results that truly move the needle.

"My mission is simple: use AI and data to give every business — big or small — the digital edge they deserve. No fluff. No fake promises. Just real, measurable growth."

— Mohit Singh, Founder & CEO

Today, RankVibe serves 150+ brands across India and the USA, delivering consistent ROI through AI-powered SEO, Google Ads, social media marketing, and content strategies that rank and convert.

When I'm not scaling businesses, I'm constantly testing new AI tools, studying Google's algorithm updates, and sharing my knowledge through this platform — because a rising tide lifts all boats.
